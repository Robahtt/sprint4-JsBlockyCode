FAQs Section

❓ Do I need programming experience to play the game?

No! The game is designed for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced coders.

❓ What happens if I get an answer wrong?

You can rearrange your code blocks and try again until you find the correct solution.

❓ Is there a way to track my progress?

Yes, your progress is saved locally in your browser, and future versions will include login-based tracking.

❓ How do I know if I completed a level successfully?

When you solve a level correctly, you’ll see a success message and a button to move to the next challenge.

❓ Can I go back and replay previous levels?

Absolutely! You can revisit any completed level to practice or improve your solution.

❓ Is JS Blocky Code free to use?

Yes! All features are free to use for learners of all levels.
